Kronos: Powerful LLMs That Stay Local

Nucore TechnologiesFebruary 2026
Summary

Kronos is Nucore's compute infrastructure for large language models. It lets a company run powerful LLMs on its own hardware and share them across teams, so people can collaborate on the same model without sending any data to an outside cloud.

The problem

The easy way to use a strong model is to send your text to someone else's servers. For many companies that is not acceptable: the prompt itself often contains private code, contracts, or customer data. But the other option, giving every team its own separate setup, is expensive and keeps teams from sharing what they learn.

Our approach

Kronos gives a company one powerful model that it owns and that many teams can use at once.

  • Stays local. The model and all data run on hardware you control. Nothing leaves your network.
  • Shared safely. Different teams use the same model, but each team's data and tuning stay private to that team.
  • Full size. It runs large, capable models, not just small cut-down ones.
  • You own it. Pay for hardware you keep instead of a monthly cloud bill that never ends.

Why it matters

Because Kronos is shared, teams can work together on one model and split the cost of the hardware, while each team keeps its own data private. You get the power of a frontier model with the privacy of running it in your own building.

Takeaway

Powerful AI does not have to live in someone else's data center. Kronos keeps strong models local, owned, and safely shared across an organization.
